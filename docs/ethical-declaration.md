# Ethical Declaration

## Defensive Purpose

AgentShield is designed exclusively for **defensive cybersecurity** purposes. It protects AI agents and organizations from malicious content attacks.

## What We Do

- Analyze incoming content for security threats
- Block and sanitize malicious prompts before they reach AI agents
- Provide security monitoring and threat analytics
- Demonstrate attack scenarios using **synthetic test data only**

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do not attack real systems, websites, networks, or third-party platforms
- We do not provide tools for offensive hacking or unauthorized access
- We do not collect or exfiltrate real user data
- We do not bypass security controls of external services

## Test Data

All attack simulations and sample data in this repository are synthetic. They are designed to demonstrate detection capabilities without targeting real individuals or systems.

## Compliance

This solution is intended for:
- Security research and education
- Organizational self-protection
- Hackathon demonstration (AIRO2026)

## Team Confirmation

We confirm that our MVP:
1. Is developed solely for defensive cybersecurity
2. Uses only synthetic/simulated attack data for demonstrations
3. Does not perform unauthorized actions on external systems
4. Complies with applicable laws and ethical guidelines
